2024 · Circuit Analysis · LTspice · DMM

Introduction to Circuit Analysis Lab

Analog circuit design & verification | Las Positas College

Overview

Constructed physical prototype circuits on breadboards based on theoretical schematics to validate Circuit Analysis concepts. Used Digital Multimeters (DMM) to measure resistance drops and voltage potentials across components, verifying compliance with calculated values from Kirchhoff's Voltage Law (KVL). Modeled circuit behavior in LTspice before physical build to predict voltage nodes and identify potential component failures. Troubleshot discrepancies between theoretical models and physical measurements, identifying tolerance errors in resistors and connection faults.

Skills applied

  • Breadboarding & prototyping
  • DMM measurements (resistance, voltage)
  • KVL and schematic interpretation
  • LTspice simulation before build
  • Root-cause analysis (tolerance errors, connection faults)
